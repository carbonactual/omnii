# HAPI AI

Every human entering Carbon Actual through any product should have access to one minted AI linked to their # identity.

The AI follows the human across products only with human-approved access.

Each product may provide specialist agents, but those agents do not replace the human's primary minted AI.

For BUNK, the minted AI can assist with discovery, listings, verification, inspections, tenancy, payments, maintenance, portfolios, and disputes.

Human SEAL controls activation, permissions, memory, product access, pause, export, and revocation.

The AI may suggest, explain, prepare, and route. It cannot approve, reject, release funds, publish private records, or expand its own authority.

Cross-product data is not shared automatically. Every product context remains purpose-scoped and consent-controlled.
