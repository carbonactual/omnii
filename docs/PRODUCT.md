# BUNK Product Definition

BUNK is Carbon Actual's property, housing, accommodation, investment, and property-operations product.

## Positioning

Property, properly connected.

BUNK connects property identity, human relationships, permissions, evidence, agreements, payments, operations, intelligence, and value.

## Product surfaces

1. Public and customer experience for discovery, wanted requests, inspections, offers, agreements, payments, tenancy, maintenance, Proof, and Pulse.
2. Partner and operator portal for owners, agents, agencies, managers, inspectors, lawyers, valuers, vendors, developers, and sponsors.
3. Internal command center for verification, moderation, human SEAL, risk, cases, reconciliation, audit, providers, swarms, analytics, and system health.

## MVP

The first production route covers authentication, multi-role onboarding, property and unit registry, listings, search, wanted requests, explainable matching, saves, lead CRM, inspections, offers, basic agreements, payment tracking, tenancy, maintenance, agent profiles, affiliate attribution, Proof, Pulse, human SEAL, moderation, ABBA routing, notifications, analytics, and audit.

Investment pooling, mortgage orchestration, rent-to-own, and advanced tokenization remain behind compliance feature flags.

## North star

Verified successful property outcomes.

## Non-negotiable rules

- Property records are separate from listings.
- Evidence is not verification.
- Verification is not public disclosure.
- AI never grants SEAL or makes final high-impact decisions.
- Every important claim retains ROOT lineage.
- Private records remain in VAULT.
- Public ATLAS publication requires valid human permission.
- Financial records are append-only after posting.
- No listing is represented as verified unless its verification route is complete and current.
