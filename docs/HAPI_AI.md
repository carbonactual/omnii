# HAPI and Minted AI Foundation Boundary

HAPI onboarding, # identity resolution, minted AI formation or restoration, and human SEAL are shared Carbon Actual foundation services.

BUNK does not create a separate HAPI identity, duplicate the human profile, mint a second primary AI, or own the cross-product relationship.

## Entry contract

1. BUNK receives a foundation session containing the resolved human # identity.
2. HAPI supplies the permitted relationship and consent context.
3. ABBA supplies the human's existing minted AI reference or completes first-time formation at the foundation layer.
4. SEAL supplies the exact BUNK scopes approved by the human.
5. BUNK activates one or more product roles and creates only BUNK-specific records.

## BUNK responsibilities

BUNK may activate product roles such as seeker, tenant, buyer, seller, owner, landlord, agent, agency member, manager, investor, vendor, inspector, reviewer, or administrator.

BUNK may expose specialist agents and tools for discovery, listings, verification support, inspections, deals, tenancy, payments, maintenance, portfolios, and redress. These remain product capabilities used by the human's minted AI or by ABBA; they are not replacements for the human's primary minted AI.

## Data boundary

BUNK stores property-domain records and product-role state. Shared human identity, minted AI ownership, and cross-product memory remain in the foundation.

Cross-product data is never copied or shared automatically. BUNK receives only purpose-limited claims and references granted through human SEAL. VAULT content remains restricted, and ATLAS publication requires separate approval.

## Authority boundary

The human remains final. AI may suggest, explain, prepare, compare, and route. It cannot approve, reject, release funds, publish protected records, sign agreements, decide disputes, or expand its own access.
